ICS 208 Safety Messsage/Plan

Purpose.

The Safety Message/Plan (ICS 208) expands on the Safety Message and Site Safety Plan.

Preparation.

The ICS 208 is an optional form that may be completed by the Safety Officer for the Incident Action Plan (IAP).

Distribution.

The ICS 208, if developed, will be reproduced with the IAP and given to all recipients as part of the IAP. All completed original forms must be given to the Documentation Unit.

Notes:
The ICS 208 may serve (optionally) as part of the IAP.
Use additional copies for continuation sheets as needed, and indicate pagination as used.
